# Climits


**Symbol Constants in Climits**
`CHAR_BIT`
> length of char  
`CHAR_MAX`
> maximum of char  
`CHAR_MIN`
> minimum of char  
`SCHAR_MAX`
> maximum of signed char  
`SCHAR_MIN`
> minimum of signed char  
`UCHAR_MAX`
> maximum of unsigned char  
`SHRT_MAX`
> maximum of short  
`SHRT_MIN`
> minimum of short  
`USHRT_MAX`
> maximum of unsigned short  
`INT_MAX`
> maximum of int  
`INT_MIN`
> minimum of int  
`UNIT_MAX`
> maximum of unsigned int  
`LONG_MAX`
> maximum of long  
`LONG_MIN`
> minimum of long  
`ULONG_MAX`
> maximum of unsigned long  
`LLONG_MAX`
> maximum of long long  
`LLONG_MIN`
> minimum of long long  
`ULLONG_MAX`
> maximum of unsigned long long
